# Scene Traversal

## Route

The viewer follows a faint spore-vein path through an ancient desert basin.

The route enters near the top of the scene and continues toward the bottom, suggesting the beginning of a journey.

## Viewer Experience

The viewer should feel like they are quietly observing the first signs of life returning to a dead world.

## Shared Visual Cue

A faint network of spore-veins slowly spreads through the sand.

This cue should remain visible across all strips.

## Strip Responsibilities

### Front Strip

Frames the route with rocks, sand, and sparse fungal growth.

### Main Strip

Shows the primary visible route through the scene.

### Rear Strip

Extends and reinforces the route.

### Atmosphere Strip

Supports the route with haze, dust, drifting spores, and distant continuity.

## Scene Rules

- Top-to-bottom progression
- Same camera distance across strips
- Same canvas size and alignment across strips
- Ancient desert mood
- Sparse fungal awakening
- No visible destination

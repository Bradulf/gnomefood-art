# Scene Traversal

## Route
The viewer follows the same fungal-vein path from Scene 002 toward the mound.

The route is one continuous side-view scene slice: cracked desert and thin veins lead toward the mound, the mound opens along the path, then the route continues past the opened earth with blue glow, spores, and new mushroom clusters carrying forward.

## Viewer Feeling
Awe and wonder with a hint of curiosity, like the viewer is watching the mound change while walking toward and past it.

## Shared Visual Cue
Thin fungal veins lead into the mound, blue bioluminescent glow emerges from the opening, and little white mushroom clusters mark the rim and continue along the route after the opening.

# 01 Front Strip

## What This Strip Shows
Route-edge cracked sand, brittle rocks, dry root fibers, and close route fragments trace the viewer's approach, the moment beside the mound, and the route continuing past it. Near the top/north, show sparse cracked sand and thin veins. Around the center, use cropped rim fragments, clustered little white mushrooms, and blue light catching dust near the opening. Near the bottom/south, continue the framing fragments with new mushrooms and faint blue glow so the strip feels like movement along the path, not a static close-up.

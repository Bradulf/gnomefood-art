# 04 Atmosphere Strip

## What This Strip Shows
Transparent dust, spores, heat shimmer, and blue bioluminescent glow carry the action along the whole path. Near the top/north, use faint dust and sparse spore flecks over the approach. Around the center, concentrate blue glow and disturbed dust around the side-view mound opening. Near the bottom/south, let the glow thin into drifting spores and soft blue haze so the viewer feels they have moved past the event while it remains alive behind them.

# 03 Rear Strip

## What This Strip Shows
Supporting terrain preserves the same mound alignment from Scene 002 while showing the route event across the full scene. Near the top/north, repeat cracked sand shelves and fungal veins from the previous scene. Around the center, support the mound opening with secondary rim shapes, small white mushroom clusters, loose dirt fragments, and subtle blue glints. Near the bottom/south, continue the terrain with repeated veins, drifting spores, and small mushroom clusters so the viewer feels the change continuing down the path. The mound reads as dirt opening into more dirt, not a tunnel, building, shrine, or doorway.

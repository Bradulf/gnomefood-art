# 02 Main Strip

## What This Strip Shows
The main side-view route depicts the whole action sequence: approach the closed mound, witness it split open, then continue past the changed earth. Near the top/north, show the cracked path and fungal veins leading toward the mound. In the center, show the mound opening from the side with lifted dirt lips, exposed inner dirt, little white mushroom clusters on the rim, and blue bioluminescent light from inside. Near the bottom/south, show the path continuing past the mound with blue-lit spores and new mushrooms carrying the change forward.

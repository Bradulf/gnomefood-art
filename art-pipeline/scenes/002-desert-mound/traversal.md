# Scene Traversal

## Route

The viewer follows the growing spore-vein path toward a strange mound emerging from the desert floor.

The route continues past the mound toward the bottom of the scene.

## Viewer Experience

The viewer should feel curiosity and wonder.

Something beneath the sand appears to be waking up.

## Shared Visual Cue

The fungal veins all lead toward the mound.

Small white mushrooms begin appearing around its edges.

## Scene Rules

- Top-to-bottom progression
- Same camera distance across strips
- Same canvas size and alignment across strips
- Ancient desert mood
- Early fungal growth
- The mound is the focal point
- No visible destination

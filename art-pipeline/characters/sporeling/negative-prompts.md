# Sporeling Negative Prompts

Use these negative instructions whenever the sporeling appears in a generated layer.

## Identity Protection

Avoid:

- Human child, elf, goblin, dwarf, robot, generic mascot, cartoon mushroom, or plush toy.
- Major changes to body proportions, cap silhouette, or core field-worker identity.
- Overly cute expression, toy-like rendering, or simplified sticker style.
- Clean superhero costume, sleek armor, or generic cyberpunk streetwear.

## Scene Integration

Avoid:

- Character rendered as part of a complete flat background.
- Extra duplicate sporelings unless the scene specifically calls for a group.
- Character appearing in the wrong parallax layer.
- Character blocking important transparent areas of the layer.
- Character scaled too large unless the scene calls for a near-layer close-up.

## Technical

Avoid:

- Text, labels, logos, UI, watermarks, borders, frames.
- Cropped-off character parts unless the layer brief requests edge framing.
- Opaque rectangular background.
- Inconsistent lighting or perspective compared with the assigned scene layer.
